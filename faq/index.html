Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Lotusforever?

Lotusforever® is an online yoga platform offering guided sessions by experienced tutors, streamed directly to your device so you can practice anytime, anywhere.

2. Do I need any experience to join a class?

No prior experience is required! Our classes are beginner-friendly, and we also offer intermediate and advanced programs to grow with you.

3. How do I sign up for a class?

Simply click on the “Sign Up” button in the navigation or visit our Sign Up/Login page to register.

4. What type of yoga classes do you offer?

We offer a range of styles including Vinyasa, Hatha, Yin Yoga, Power Yoga, and Mindful Meditation, with flexible weekly schedules.

5. Can I choose specific tutors?

Yes, you can view our available tutors and select classes based on their specialties and teaching times.

6. Are the classes live or pre-recorded?

We currently offer both live-streamed sessions and pre-recorded classes for on-demand access to fit your schedule.

7. Do I need special equipment?

A yoga mat is ideal, but beginners can start with just comfortable clothing and a quiet space. Props like blocks or straps are optional.

8. Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial to explore classes and meet our instructors before choosing a membership.

9. How do I cancel or change my subscription?

You can manage your plan anytime from your account settings. We also offer flexible 3, 6, or 12-month plans.

10. Can I access classes on mobile devices?

Absolutely. Lotusforever is mobile-friendly and works seamlessly across smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
